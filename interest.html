I love movies!

I have many different interests including video games, coding and sports. However, my favourite way to spend my free time is by watching movies. I love all genres of movies but my favourite genres are thriller, suspense and science fiction. My favourite directors are Quentin Tarantino, Martin Scorsese and Christopher Nolan.
